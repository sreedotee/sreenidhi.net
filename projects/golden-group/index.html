Golden Group Web Design

Golden Group is a real-estate developer working across two divisions: Residential and Commercial & Industrial. We designed and built their website to give buyers and stakeholders a clear way into each division and the projects within it.

A real-estate website is rarely where a buyer makes the decision, but it's almost always where they decide whether something is worth a conversation with sales. We treated every page as evidence: enough imagery, enough specifics, enough context for a serious buyer to feel they've seen what they need before reaching out, with a clear contact path the moment they're ready.

The homepage splits cleanly into the two divisions, each leading into its own catalog. The Projects page itself is a filterable list, so a returning visitor can jump straight to what they care about, while the divisional routes give first-time visitors a guided way in.

Each project page leads with imagery, then layers in the specifics a buyer evaluates: typology, key details, location, and a direct route to sales. The same template carries residential developments and commercial buildings, keeping the catalog consistent without forcing different kinds of projects into the same voice.

The work covered both design and development, delivered as a three-person team. The site is fully responsive across mobile, tablet, and desktop.
