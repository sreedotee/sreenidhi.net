Golden Group Web Design

Golden Group is a real-estate brand whose website I designed in 2025. The brief was to translate the company's portfolio and process into something that felt as considered as the buildings themselves — restrained, exact, and unmistakably theirs.

Restraint over decoration. Type hierarchy and a small palette carry the brand; imagery does the rest. Every page treats white space as a feature, not a gap — the work for the visitor is recognizing intent, not parsing layout.
