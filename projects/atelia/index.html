Atelia Product Design

Atelia is a conceptual digital shopping companion designed to eliminate hesitation at checkout. By offering a seamless Virtual Try-On/Off experience, it brings the reassurance of a fitting room online. Users can simply paste a product link from any store and instantly visualize how the garment looks on their own body.

Beyond visualizing the fit, Atelia serves as a centralized hub for planning future purchases. Shoppers can save the items they try on, organize them into curated collections, and easily compare different styling options before deciding to buy. By providing this clarity before checkout, the product turns an uncertain online shopping experience into an engaging and highly personalized process.
