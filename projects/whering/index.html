Whering Redesign

Whering is a wardrobe app I redesigned end-to-end using formal concept analysis. The original product had eight screens, but its concepts overlapped: Canvas and Dress Me did the same job, Calendar and Planner duplicated each other, and Add From Wardrobe appeared three navigation levels deep. The redesign treats those collisions as the real problem to solve, not a styling exercise.

The redesign collapses the overlapping screens into eight orthogonal concepts — Item, Collection, Outfit, Style, Planner, Event, Discover, Profile — and reroutes navigation around what users actually do. Eight concept synchronizations were mapped formally before any screen was drawn. The final navigation (Discover · Search · Style · Planner · Profile) is the resolved cluster, not the surface inventory of the old app.
