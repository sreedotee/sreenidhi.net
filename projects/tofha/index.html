Tofha Web Design

Tofha is a recent landing site — brand-forward, built to make the company's offer legible inside fifteen seconds. A single-page architecture where type, pacing, and rhythm do most of the work, and the visitor moves through the entire pitch in one scroll.

The design constraint was forced restraint — every section had to earn its keep on a single page. The result reads more like a piece of writing than a brochure: hierarchy carries meaning, white space carries weight, and the visitor finishes the page knowing what the company does and why.
